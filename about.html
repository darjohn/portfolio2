Hello!

I am a graphic designer living in the San Francisco Bay Area. I design for print, web, and videos, working across a wide range of disciplines including typography, illustration, layout, identity, and motion.

I graduated with a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree in Visual Communication from the Herron School of Art and Design at Indiana University—Purdue University Indianapolis. After graduating, I moved to the Bay Area to pursue my passion in design. I am very motivated and love finding new solutions to projects. In my free time you can usually find me designing, skateboarding, snowboarding, biking, woodworking, taking pictures, traveling, or watching TV.
